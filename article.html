Analogy on the Use of AI in Dev Work

You are organizing your wedding, the documentation team is asking 40k for the wedding package. They will use an Iphone 16 pro max to cover the event and CapCut for the same day edit video. The documentation team has 2 years of experience and has an above average portfolio. Would you agree to that deal? Explain your answer and justify the integrity of their work. Reflect on the integrity, creativity, skill level of the “vibe” coders.

I would only agree to the 40k package if I can see with my own eyes that the team’s work is really good. Even if they are only using an iPhone 16 Pro Max and CapCut, I believe that what matters most is the collaborative skills, not the tool they use. Plus, they already have 2 years of experience and an above- average portfolio, so if their videos look clean and well edited, then I will agree that the 40k package is worth it. This can be compared to vibe coders. They are coders that don’t write code manually, all they do is tell the AI what they want, and the AI does the code. With proper prompts to the AI, they can make superb codes. I believe that their creativity also contributes to the result of the AI. I just hope that they use AI the right way.
