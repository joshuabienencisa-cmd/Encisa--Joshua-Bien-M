Ethical Use of AI

Responsible use in the academe

Students can always use GenAI to have inspiration, explain hard terms, make drafts, and also make reviewers, but the student’s final work must still be their own. Since, if they solely used GenAI in their work, the teacher might feel disrespected and hurt. Teachers should always have an AI checker to check their work

Avoiding plagiarism and misuse

To avoid plagiarism, students should rewrite, understand, or paraphrase the answers given by GenAI instead of the copy and paste method. GenAI can help, but there are limits with the use of it.

Accuracy and verification of AI outputs

GenAI’s are not always correct. So , students must always double check facts using reliable sources. They should not 100% rely with the output of GenAI’s, though some of them state their references, it does not take a lot of effort to delve into the given information and double check them.

Bias and fairness

Sometimes, GenAI can produce biased statements that students might offend other people by copying GenAI’s work. People should think carefully about GenAI’s answers to avoid sharing anything harmful.

My own recommended guidelines

1. GenAI’s are just tools to help you, not the final answers 2. Double check facts using reliable sources not just fully relying on GenAI 3. If given permission to use GenAI by your teacher, always follow their rules on how to use them 4. Always use AI honestly.
